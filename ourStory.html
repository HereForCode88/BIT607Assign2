Our Story

About the Owners

The owners of Haukai Restaurant, located in the picturesque Bay of Islands, are dedicated entrepreneurs with a passion for creating a unique dining experience. They have built their restaurant business from the ground up, focusing on offering a blend of flavors and cultures that cater to both locals and tourists.

Currently, the restaurant operates in the evenings from Wednesday to Sunday, serving a diverse menu that combines traditional Māori and Pākeha cuisine, complemented by a selection of beverages. The owners are not just culinary enthusiasts; they are also keen on incorporating elements of Māori culture into their restaurant experience. This includes practices like karakia, which blesses the food and adds cultural depth to the dining experience.

About the Restaurant

The Haukai Restaurant is a charming establishment nestled in the heart of the Bay of Islands. Our restaurant is designed to provide a warm and welcoming atmosphere where guests can enjoy exceptional cuisine and a memorable dining experience.

With breathtaking views of the bay and a commitment to using locally sourced ingredients, we aim to showcase the beauty and flavors of the Taranaki region. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply looking for a delightful meal, our restaurant is the perfect destination.

Food and Culture

At Haukai, we take pride in our commitment to both culinary excellence and the rich Māori culture of New Zealand. Our menu reflects this dedication by offering a fusion of traditional Māori and Pākeha cuisine.

We believe that food is not only a feast for the palate but also a celebration of heritage. That's why we incorporate cultural elements into our dining experience, such as karakia (blessings) that honor our ingredients and traditions.

Join us in exploring the flavors of the region while immersing yourself in the vibrant culture that inspires our culinary creations.
