About Us

At Haukai, our culinary passion revolves around showcasing the finest regional ingredients sourced right here in the Taranaki region. Our culinary philosophy is grounded in a commitment to freshness and locality, utilizing the highest quality multicultural ingredients. We offer an enticing and contemporary bistro menu that caters to a wide range of tastes, ensuring there's something delightful for every palate. Combined with our warm and attentive service, we eagerly look forward to extending our hospitality to you and your loved ones at Haukai Bistro & Bar in the near future.
